Community Protocol

LET'S MAKE LIFE BETTER

🏢 COMMON AREAS

✅ Do's

  • Respect everybody's right, dignity and privacy
  • Maintain cleanliness & calm
  • Use dustbins
  • Don't keep garbage outside the flat once housekeeping staff has collected it
  • Help neighbours
  • Teach your child to refrain himself/herself from playing such outdoor games that prone to damage others property like window panes/cars etc.

❌ Don'ts

  • Don't occupy common spaces like lobbies, staircases with own belongings
  • Don't throw things out of window
  • Don't disturb neighbours by creating noise/ playing sound system loudly
  • Don't drop water from wet clothes or plants
  • Don't allow children play beside parking spaces

🛗 LIFTS / ELEVATORS

✅ Do's

  • Use properly
  • Maintain cleanliness
  • Ensure safety

❌ Don'ts

  • Don't scratch or damage
  • Don't use incase of fire incident
  • Don't forget to switch off the fan if you are the last person to leave

🐕 PETS

✅ Do's

  • Pets should have valid license from KMC
  • Should be duly vaccinated
  • Should be musked & harnessed /on-leashed in public places
  • Should use Service Lifts to carry pets

❌ Don'ts

  • No peeing or pooping in common areas else please clean
  • Pets shouldn't be left alone for long hours

🔥 FIRE SAFETY

✅ Do's

  • Be aware of fire safety
  • Be more careful about burning gas and puja flames
  • Periodically check Gas gadgets
  • Use staircase in case of fire
  • Be aware about the nearest Fire Refuge
  • Know the usage of Fire fighting equipments
  • Keep the lobby & staircase free for emergency movement

❌ Don'ts

  • Don't panic
  • Don't use lift in case of Fire
  • Don't keep gas cylinders and inflammable items in the lobbies and staircases

🚗 PARKING

✅ Do's

  • All four wheelers and two wheelers should have proper stickers with parking space number
  • Cars without stickers will be denied entry
  • Keep cars in proper/ alloted proper space
  • Take permission for guest parking prior through mail

📋 TRANSFER OF OWNERSHIP

  • Always inform the Association office through writing, whoever sells or buys or otherwise transfers his apartment

🔨 REPAIR / CIVIL WORKS

✅ Do's

  • Damages occurred in common areas, lobbies only will be repaired by the Association /Board
  • Should inform the Association office for renovation or modification inside the apartment

❌ Don'ts

  • No structural change can be done which can cause threat to the building
